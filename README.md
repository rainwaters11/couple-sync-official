## 🎯 4-Line Problem Frame
* **User:** Busy couples struggling to maintain consistent, balanced emotional connection.
* **Problem:** Relationship maintenance—often called "invisible emotional labor"—frequently falls disproportionately on one partner (statistically women) and lacks mutual visibility.
* **Constraints:** Couples are burned out on screens and need simple, low-barrier actions that don't feel like long therapy checklists.
* **Success Test:** Both partners complete a 7-day streak of synchronized, screen-free rituals to unlock a shared, real-world reward stored on an immutable ledger.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
**Couple Sync** is a lightweight, decentralized relationship ritual app designed to help partners stay emotionally connected through small daily actions. The dApp addresses **UN SDG 5 (Gender Equality)** by taking the "invisible" emotional labor of maintaining a relationship and making it visible, equitable, and rewarding through a shared on-chain ledger.

Instead of clinical checklists, partners secretly plan a weekend surprise and unlock daily clues by completing their connection rituals. Consistency is the real romance.

## ✨ Key Features
* **The Mutual Gift (Weekly Surprise):** Each week runs Saturday-to-Friday. Partners secretly select a date or experience. 
* **The Cross-Hint System:** Completing daily check-ins unlocks progressive clues about the partner's secret plan, making emotional equity visible and engaging.
* **Privacy-First Architecture:** Relationship data is highly sensitive. Devices sync via a shared room code. 
* **Zero-Knowledge Local Fallback:** If the blockchain network or Motoko backend experiences latency, the app gracefully falls back to local storage, ensuring couples are never blocked from their daily check-in.
* **The 7-Day Reveal Ceremony:** On Friday (Day 7), the app reveals the secret weekend plans.

## 🗺️ Web3 Roadmap: Soulbound Tokens (SBTs)
Couple Sync tracks 20-day connection streaks to unlock Monthly Badges (e.g., "Luck of Love," "New Year Devotion"). We are actively integrating the **ICRC-7 NFT Standard** to mint these milestones as **Soulbound Tokens**. Because these tokens are non-transferable, they create a permanent, decentralized, and highly personal on-chain gallery of a couple's authentic relationship milestones directly in their ICP wallets.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
* **Backend / Smart Contracts:** Motoko
* **Blockchain Network:** Internet Computer Protocol (ICP)
* **Storage:** LocalStorage (Front-end state caching) & ICP Canisters (Shared Room State)
* **Prototyping Tooling:** Caffeine.ai
